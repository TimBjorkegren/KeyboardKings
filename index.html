Our Switches

Mechanical switches are the heart of a mechanical keyboard, providing a distinct feel and sound with each keystroke. Unlike membrane keyboards, mechanical switches use individual mechanisms for each key, allowing for greater durability and customization. They come in various types, such as linear, tactile, and clicky, each offering a unique typing experience.
